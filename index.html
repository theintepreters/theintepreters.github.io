My story begin with my name "Tran". It is interesting that the word "tran" can combine with many other word conveying different meanings. It can mean a shameless gaze that we all used to have; it can mean the respect for our loved ones; or it can mean the pearl gem or the boba in the glass of boba tea.

That's it for the name. The rest of myself will be revealed through many special projects of my own.
